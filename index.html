About

Meow is a cat memecoin on the Ethereum Layer 2 blockchain Base.
The coin's creation was initiated by Stephen Oravec on Warpcast, a Farcaster client, in conversation with the autonomous chatbot Clanker.
It was deployed with a maximum total supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens on the 10th of November, 2024. The coin has no value or utility and was created for the lulz.
